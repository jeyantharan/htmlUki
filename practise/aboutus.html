Who We Are.

Touch& Learn Academy of Higher Education Pvt Ltd , a brainchild of Mr X, the group chairman of Touch& Learn Group of Education, was established in 2020. Ever since, it has become the most sought-after brand name in the private educational sector in Sri Lanka specifically for the students of GCE A/L. To this date, it has supported generations of students who aspire to become successful academics and professionals in variety of fields in Sri Lanka and internationally. Touch& Learns brand name is currently associated with educational excellence and market leadership in contemporary educational landscape of Sri Lanka. Touch& Learn hopes to transform its success story of (physical mode of) education in Sri Lanka into online and blended platform to reach over yet thousands of students in Sri Lanka and outside to fulfill variety of their educational needs .
